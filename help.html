Need Help Entering a Recipe?

Before starting your recipe, you must be logged in. If not logged in, an alert will send you to the "Sign In" page to login or create an account. Once you log in and return to the Create Recipe page, you can click "Hide Navigation" to collapse the navigation menu to save space. Click again to restore the full navigation menu.

To begin, enter the name of your recipe, then choose a Category and Type. Enter a description and number of servings if desired. Click "Save Recipe Information" to get started.

A carefully curated list of common ingredients will appear with the most common ingredients based on the category and type selections you made when creating your recipe. These ingredients are culled from 10,000 recipes with most common appearing at the top of the list. Click an ingredient, then use the buttons to choose quantities. For instance, for 1-1/2 Cups of the ingredient, click "Cup:, then "1/2 Cup". If you want two cups, click "Cup" twice.

As you are adding quantities, the quantities and the name of the ingredient appear. If the cook needs to split an ingredient during preparation, click "Divided" and it will display properly in the recipe.

When you are finished with the quantity buttons, click Add. The ingredient is added to your recipe.To make it easier to locate common ingredients in the main list, the ingredient you added is removed.

If you need an ingredient that is not readily available on the list of curated ingredients, type a search term in the search box. MyRecipEase will search it's entire database of ingredients, regardless of which category or type you selected, giving you access to hundreds and hundreds more ingredients. Once the list appears, click the ingredient you wish, and click the quantity buttons as before.

Should you enter the wrong ingredient, or the wrong quantity, click the ingredient in the Entered Ingredients list, and click "Delete". The ingredient will be restored to the list of available ingredients above.

Once you have entered an ingredient, you can then enter instruction steps. Simply type the instruction and click "Submit". You can enter as many steps as you like, there's no limit.

As with the ingredients, if you have an unneeded instruction, or make a mistake in one, simply click it and click "Delete" to remove it.

Once your recipe is complete, click "Save Recipe" to save your full recipe to the database. If you decide to discard your recipe at any time, just click "Clear Recipe" and confirm your wishes, and it will be deleted. That's it!
